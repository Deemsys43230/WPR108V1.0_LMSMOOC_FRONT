Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is Learnterest?

Learnterest is an Online Learning Platform established for the welfare of both Instructors and the learners. Instructor can make the most of our platform Learnterest to publish their course and teach online. Virtual learners who are keen in enhancing their skills and careers can avail the apt course and enroll themselves in it.

Who all can use Learnterest?

Anyone with quality content and ability to educate the learners can make use of the Learnterest platform. As an Instructor you can benefit yourself by achieving a great satisfaction in teaching courses to your students and get paid for your effort.

What should I do if I forgot my username/password?

The log-in page will have an option stating 'forgot password/ forgot username' on clicking that link you will receive an email to reset your password.

I tried to register, but it says the username is already taken.

The user name is unique and the system only allows it to be used once. Try a different, more exclusive user name.

Does Internet required to use Learnterest?

Yes, Internet is required to enroll and continue to learn the courses.

Is Learnterest available worldwide?

Yes, Learnterest is open for global audience and it can be used worldwide irrespective of your location.

What if I have more issues at any phase?

You are free to e-mail us at support_mooc@deemsysinc.com

Does Learnterest support all kind of browsers?

Yes, Learnterest is highly compatible and is available for mobile, tablet users too.

Instructors

How do I enroll myself as an Instructor?

To join up as an instructor visit the sign up page endowed for the Instructor, update your profile and upload your course.

Why aren't my courses showing up?

The course content you have uploaded will be reviewed by the team Learnterest to provide quality content for the users. It will take 24-72 business hours to review and publish the content in Learnterest.

Instructors are advised not to use any kind of offensive/ abusive words in the content they upload.

What are the types of contents I can publish?

You can upload your courses via text, video and audio. The online streaming will be introduced shortly where you can also organize webinars, online sessions etc.

Can I publish more than one course?

Yes, you can publish more than one course either as free or paid using a single login.

Can I change my free course as paid?

Yes, you can change your free course to paid course. Send a request to the team Learnterest and after reviewing it the price will be changed from free to paid course.

Can I edit/delete my course after publishing?

No, you cannot edit/delete your course after publishing as it may affect the readers. You can send a request for edit/ delete if it is most needed.

Does Learnterest support online video streaming?

Online video streaming option, webinar, discussion forums will be introduced shortly

Can I be a student with the same login ID?

No, you cannot. You have to create a separate Log in ID as a Student.

What is Promo code?

Promo code is applied during the online purchase of the course at the time of bill payment; it allows the student to avail an additional discount on the course enrolled. Promo code has to be set by the instructor if he wishes to give any discount for his students.

What are the types of Promo code?

Public & Private promo codes are the two types of promo code available with Learnterest. Public promo code is displayed for all learners whereas the private promo codes can be sent by the Instructor to his personal circle and friends as a promotional offer.

How will Learnterest support me if I face any problem?

At Learnterest we sort out your issues within 48 business hours. For any assistance kindly send an e-mail to support_mooc@deemsysinc.com

What is the difference between promo video and Course video?

Promo video is the short description about the entire course and the course video is the detailed description about the entire course and its sub categories

How will I receive my payment?

After each successful enrollment of the students for your course, you will be receiving your percentage share through PayPal.

Do I need to create a PayPal account?

Yes, you need to create a PayPal account for receiving your payments.

How to create PayPal account?

You can create your PayPal account using visa/master debit & credit cards. Visit the below mentioned link and sign up for creating your own PayPal account.

How do I promote my course?

You can use social media and promo code for promoting your course. You can also provide your course for free and after enrollment of more than sufficient students you can change it as paid course.

Will Learnterest help me in promoting my course?

Yes Learnterest will help promoting your course.
